Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

You can book a service through our website by clicking "Book Now" and filling out the service request form, or by calling us directly at 0550697670.

Are your professionals insured?

Yes, all our professionals are fully insured and bonded for your protection and peace of mind.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve Greater Accra, Kumasi, Tamale, and Takoradi. We're expanding to more cities across Ghana.

How much do services cost?

Pricing varies by service type and complexity. We provide transparent quotes before any work begins, with no hidden fees.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the service, contact us within 24 hours and we'll make it right.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we offer emergency services for urgent plumbing and electrical issues. Additional charges may apply for after-hours service.
