About FixMate

Connecting Ghanaians with trusted home service professionals

About Content

Your Trusted Home Services Partner

FixMate is Ghana's leading platform for connecting homeowners with skilled, verified professionals for all their home service needs. We understand the challenges of finding reliable service providers, and we're here to make that process simple, safe, and affordable.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to transforming how Ghanaians access home services. Our platform brings together the best local professionals, ensuring quality work and customer satisfaction across all major cities in Ghana.

Our Mission

To provide Ghanaians with easy access to reliable, professional home services while supporting local skilled workers and growing the service economy across Ghana.

Our Vision

To become Ghana's most trusted home services platform, setting the standard for quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction in the service industry.

Our Core Values

Trust & Safety

All our professionals are thoroughly vetted and background-checked to ensure your safety and peace of mind.

Quality Service

We maintain high standards by working only with skilled professionals who deliver exceptional results.

Reliability

Punctual service delivery and consistent communication to keep you informed throughout the process.

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our priority. We go above and beyond to ensure you're happy with every service.

Community Support

Supporting local Ghanaian professionals and contributing to the growth of our communities.

Fair Pricing

Transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden fees. Quality service at affordable rates.
